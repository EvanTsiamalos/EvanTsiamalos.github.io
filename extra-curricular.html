Cretan Swing Shots

This is a self-organized swing dance team at the university of Crete. It is mostly consisted of University students but it is open to people of all ages and background. The team offers free lindy hop and jazz classes at the Student Center of the university of Crete campus, in Heraklion

I am part of the organizing team and an active member.
